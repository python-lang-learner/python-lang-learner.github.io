For Loops

for loops iterate over sequences like lists, strings, tuples, sets and dictionaries.

Basic iteration

for x in [1, 2, 3]:
    print(x)

Looping strings

for ch in "python":
    print(ch)

Looping with range

for i in range(1, 6):
    print(i)

Looping dictionaries

student = {"name": "Asha", "age": 20}

for k, v in student.items():
    print(k, v)
