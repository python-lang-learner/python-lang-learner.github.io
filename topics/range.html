Range

range() creates a sequence of integers commonly used with loops.

Usage

for i in range(5):
    print(i)     # prints 0 1 2 3 4

for i in range(2, 8):
    print(i)     # 2..7

for i in range(10, 0, -2):
    print(i)     # 10 8 6 4 2 (step -2)

Range & lists

print(list(range(5)))    # [0,1,2,3,4]

Exercises

  1. Use range() to print even numbers from 2 to 20.
  2. Use range to create a list of square numbers for 1..10.
